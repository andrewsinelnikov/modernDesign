Who We Are

With over 20 years experience, Modern Design, manufactures and sells a wide range of products for the furniture, carpentry, hardware. Therefore, we cooperate with leading manufacturers.

We offer the best quality and functionality at the best price. We work to exceed consumer expectations and aim to establish long-term relationships with our customers. We advise our customers on the best solutions for furniture fittings and accessories.

We promote the use of eco-packaging on all of our products to ensure that the products used in the packages are designed to use the minimum possible amount of material, to be as small and light as possible and to be 100% recyclable.

Our managers are exited to work with you to create your design.
